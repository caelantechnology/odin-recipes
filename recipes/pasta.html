Pasta

Description

The pasta is a homemade recipe pasted down over generations. It requires little prep time and is very tasty.


Ingredients
  • Pasta noodles
  • Tomatoes, crushed
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Steps
  1. Add olive oil to pan and add peeled and crushed garlic.
  2. After that has cooked a little, add can of tomatoes.
  3. Reduce the tomatos until desired consistency, adding salt & pepper as needed.
  4. Cook pasta based on the instructions.
  5. Before pasta is fully cooked, add to sauce and cook on low.
